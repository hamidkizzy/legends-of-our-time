World Leaders

George Washington

The first President of the United States (1789–1797). Known as the "Father of His Country."

Nelson Mandela

The first black President of South Africa (1994–1999). A global icon for peace and reconciliation.

Barack Obama

The 44th President of the United States (2009–2017). The first African-American president.

Vladimir Putin

President of Russia (2000–2008, 2012–present). A prominent global political figure.

Angela Merkel

Chancellor of Germany (2005–2021). Known for her steady leadership in Europe.

Joe Biden

46th President of the United States (2021–present). Focuses on unity and rebuilding alliances.

Fidel Castro

Leader of Cuba (1959–2008). A central figure in the Cuban Revolution.

Golda Meir

Prime Minister of Israel (1969–1974). One of the first female heads of government.

Frank-Walter Steinmeier

President of Germany (2017–present). Promotes democracy and multilateralism.
